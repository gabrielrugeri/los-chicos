# UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) that match out project

SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities and Communities

This goal aims to make cities more inclusive, safe, and sustainable. An application that connects people to play sports in streets, parks, and public courts helps activate these urban spaces and encourages healthier use of the city.

• Target 11.7: Ensure universal access to safe, inclusive, and accessible public spaces.
• Target 11.3: Promote inclusive and participatory urban planning.

SDG 10 — Reduced Inequalities

Street sports are generally accessible and low-cost. An application that connects players makes it easier for people from different social backgrounds to participate, creating equal opportunities for leisure and social integration.

• Target 10.2: Promote the social, economic, and political inclusion of all people.
• Target 10.3: Ensure equal opportunity and reduce inequalities.

SDG 16 — Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

Sport can strengthen social relationships and promote cooperation among people from different groups. By creating meeting spaces through informal games, the application can contribute to more connected and collaborative communities.

• Target 16.7: Ensure inclusive decision-making and community participation.
• Target 16.b: Promote non-discriminatory policies and practices.
